Sewing Course

A beginner's sewing course is designed to introduce individuals to the art and skill of sewing, providing a solid foundation for creating and repairing clothing and fabric items. Whether you're a complete novice or someone looking to refresh their skills, this course covers the basics of sewing techniques, machine operation, and pattern reading.

Course Content

  • Introduction to Sewing
  • Sewing Machine Basics
  • Hand Sewing Techniques
  • Reading Patterns
  • Fabric Cutting and Preparation
  • Basic Sewing Projects
  • Introduction to Garment Construction
  • Zippers and Buttons

Course Price: R1500

Duration

he duration of a beginner's sewing course can vary, but it typically spans several weeks with classes held once or twice a week. This structure allows participants to practice skills between sessions.
