Landscaping Course

An advanced landscape design and management course is tailored for individuals with a foundational understanding of landscaping who wish to deepen their knowledge and skills in creating, planning, and maintaining landscapes. This course delves into advanced design principles, project management, and sustainable practices to equip participants for more complex and large-scale landscaping projects.

Course Content

  • Digital Design Tools
  • Ecological Landscaping
  • Planting Design and Maintenance Strategies
  • Hardscape Construction and Materials
  • Water Management and Irrigation Systems
  • Site Engineering and Grading
  • Project Management for Landscaping
  • Advanced Planting Techniques

Course Price: R1500

Duration

The duration of an advanced landscape design and management course can range from several weeks to a few months, depending on the intensity and depth of the content.
