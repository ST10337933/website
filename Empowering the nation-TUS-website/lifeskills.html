Life Skills Course

A life skills development course is designed to empower individuals with the essential skills necessary for personal growth, effective communication, and successful navigation of various life challenges. This comprehensive course aims to enhance participants' abilities to manage emotions, make informed decisions, build positive relationships, and achieve a balanced and fulfilling life.

Course Content

  • Self-Awareness
  • Effective Communication
  • Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving
  • Time Management
  • Stress Management
  • Emotional Intelligence
  • Financial Literacy
  • Health and Wellness

Course Price: R1500

Duration

The duration of a life skills development course can vary, but it is often structured to cover the content over several weeks or months, with regular sessions to allow for practice and reflection.
