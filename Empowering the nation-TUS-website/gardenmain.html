Garden Maintenance Course

A Garden Maintenance and Landscaping course are designed for individuals interested in acquiring practical skills and knowledge to effectively maintain and enhance outdoor spaces. Whether you're a homeowner looking to improve your own garden or someone considering a career in landscaping, this course covers essential techniques for garden upkeep and enhancement.

Course Content

  • Plant Identification and Selection
  • Soil Health and Fertilization
  • Pruning and Trimming
  • Weeding and Pest Management
  • Water Management and Irrigation Systems
  • Lawn Care
  • Seasonal Planting and Maintenance
  • Irrigation Systems

Course Price: R750

Duration

The duration of an advanced landscape design and management course can range from several weeks to a few months, depending on the intensity and depth of the content.
