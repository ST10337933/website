Child Minding Course

A Child Minding and Child Care Basics course is designed to provide individuals with the fundamental knowledge and skills required to safely and effectively care for children. This course is suitable for parents, caregivers, and individuals interested in pursuing a career in childcare. It covers various aspects of child development, safety, and age-appropriate activities

Course Content

  • Child Development
  • Safety and First Aid for Children
  • Nutrition and Healthy Snacks
  • Effective Communication with Children
  • Basic Child Psychology
  • Creating Engaging and Educational Activities
  • Child Care Laws and Regulations
  • Emergency Preparedness

Course Price: R750

Duration

The duration of a Child Minding and Child Care Basics course can vary, with some courses offered as short workshops and others spanning several weeks. The structure may include a combination of theory, practical exercises, and observation.
